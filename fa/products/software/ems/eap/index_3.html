EcoAdviser Utilization Support Services

An after-sales service for customers who are unsure of how to create a dashboard or do not have enough time after purchasing EcoAdviser, which provides support for promoting energy conservation by assisting with the creation of effective dashboards for more effective use of measurement data.
Customers with concerns about not properly utilizing EcoAdviser are advised to contact their store of purchase.

* Precautions

  • This analysis service is intended to assist customers to make more effective use of our products, and does not guarantee improvements in energy conservation.
  • Depending on the data provided, the requested dashboard design might not be available. (Additional measurements, etc. will be proposed to enhance content)
