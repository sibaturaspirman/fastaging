Main Unit

Energy visualization

Energy measuring unit
(Standard energy measurement model)

EMU4-BM1-MB

  1. Equipped with basic power measurement functions for monitoring voltage, current, power, and electric energy
  2. MODBUS RTU communication

Standard model functions plus additional functions for three-phase 4-wire measurement and pulse counting

Energy measuring unit
(High-performance energy measurement model)

EMU4-HM1-MB

  1. Same basic functions as standard model
  2. MODBUS RTU communication
  3. Three-phase 4-wire, 440 V direct voltage input available
  4. Can display harmonic current, voltage, apparent power, periodic electric energy, and electric energy conversion value
  5. Pulse/contact input/output support

Low-voltage circuit leakage current measurement

Energy measuring unit
(Insulation monitoring model)

EMU4-LG1-MB

  1. Leakage current measurement
  2. MODBUS RTU communication
  3. Ior (resistive leakage current) measurement
  4. Contact output

Equipment control based on measured values and schedules

Control unit

EMU4-CNT-MB

  1. Control output based on control judgment
  2. MODBUS RTU communication
  3. Contact output (3 points) and analog output (1 point) for equipment control applications

Extension Unit

Measurement of circuits of the same voltage

Energy measuring unit
(Energy measurement extension unit for systems with the same voltage)

EMU4-A2

  1. Measurement of two circuits (per unit)
  2. Output of the same number of contacts or pulses as the number of circuits (per circuit)
  3. No voltage connection wiring necessary for measurement of the same voltage (Measurement of same voltage as measured by unit connected on left side)

Measurement of circuits of different voltages

Energy measuring unit
(Energy measurement extension unit for systems with different voltages)

EMU4-VA2

  1. Measurement of two circuits (per unit)
  2. Output of the same number of contacts or pulses as the number of circuits (per circuit)
  3. Measurement of different transformer systems for each unit (Measurement of voltage different from that measured by unit connected on left side)

Temperature, humidity, and vibration measurement

Analog input unit

EMU4-AX4

  1. Four analog data inputs
  2. Measurement at cycles of 1 ms × number of channels or 50 ms × number of channels (configurable)
  3. Contact output
  4. Moving average (average over a specified period) calculation

Production volume and flow rate (of water, gas, or air) monitoring, equipment operation monitoring

Pulse input unit

EMU4-PX4

  1. Four pulse or contact outputs (Configurable between pulse input or contact input for each input channel)
  2. Monitoring of equipment operation time with contact input
  3. Contact output

*1：The EMU4-A2 acquires measurement point voltage values from the unit on the left side, so the EMU4-CNT-MB and EMU4-VA2 are necessary when used in combination with the EMU-CNT-MB.

Optional Unit

Automated measurement data collection/logging

Logging unit

EMU4-LM

Logging data from measured values of various measurement items (wattage, voltage, current, etc.) can be saved to SD cards as CSV files

Wide variety of transmission/communication connections

CC-Link communication unit

EMU4-CM-C

Easy extension to an EcoWebServerIII visualization system or PLC system via CC-Link communication unit

Wide variety of transmission/communication connections

CC-Link IE field network basic communication unit

EMU4-CM-CIFB

Communication of data to a PLC via Ethernet connection (LAN) with a CC-Link IE field network basic communication unit
