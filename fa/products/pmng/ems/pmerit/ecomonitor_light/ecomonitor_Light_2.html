Easy Data Collection

MODBUS RTU communication for simplified energy management

Take advantage of remote, real-time data collection

*1：“LAN ⇔ RS-485 converter” or “USB ⇔ RS-485 converter”

Data Acquisition Software (EMU4-SW1)

  • Collection of measurement data from up to 31 units (max. 124 items) in cycles of 1 minute or 1 hour
  • No need for optional units thanks to MODBUS RTU communication (standard for energy measuring units), which allows for low-cost system construction

PLC Connectivity

MODBUS RTU (RS-485) communication connectivity*1

*1：To connect to a PLC, the PLC must be equipped with a unit capable of MODBUS RTU (RS-485) communication.

CC-Link communication connectivity*2

*2：To connect to a PLC, the PLC must be equipped with a unit capable of CC-Link communication.

CC-Link IE field network basic communication connectivity

Data communication is possible using the same network (Ethernet) as factory automation devices installed on the equipment.

The data can be transmitted over the same network (Ethernet) as factory automation (FA) devices installed for the equipment. This helps reduce time spent commissioning production facilities and machines, installation costs, and wiring work.
