Vision Sensor Features Vision Sensor VS80

  • Product information

Compact vision with reduced wiring fits anywhere

Ultra-compact-size sensors (31 mm × 31 mm × 75 mm) can be installed at the maximum angle of 45 degrees. The VS80 integrates into tight spaces in the machinery, hard-to-reach spaces between machines, and on the robot hand.

PoE*1 minimizes cabling

The sensor with PoE can be connected with one dedicated cable, minimizing cabling.

*1.Power Over Ethernet

Working distance

The distance from a lens to an inspection target is referred to as "working distance", and an area where a vision sensor can see at that distance is referred to as "field of view".
