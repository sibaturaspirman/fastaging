Announcement on the end of repair service of the MELSEC-A/QnA (large type) Series

In September 2006, we have discontinued the MELSEC-A/QnA Series, which have received support for about 20 years since its release in 1985. Service availability period was seven years after product discontinuation. The service ended in the end of September 2013. Please consider replacing it with the MELSEC iQ-R Series.

Announcement on the end of repair service availability of the MELSEC-AnS/QnAs (small type) Series and MELSEC-I/OLINK

In the end of September 2014, we have discontinued the MELSEC-AnS/QnAS (small type) Series and MELSEC-I/OLINK, which have received support for about 20 years since its release in 1990. Service availability period is seven years after product discontinuation. The service ended in the end of September 2021. Please consider replacing them with the MELSEC iQ-R Series and AnyWire.
