MELSEC-F series Parallel Link

  • 製品情報

Features

  • 1.Data link between two FX PLC main units can be made easily just by programming to specify master/slave station.
  • 2.Bit devices (50 to 100 points) and word devices (10 points) are automatically linked between each station. Depending on the devices allocated in the host station, the ON/OFF states and data register values of the other station can be known.
