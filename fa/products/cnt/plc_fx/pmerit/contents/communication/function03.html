MELSEC-F series N:N Network

Features

  1. 1.Data link can be realized by a simple program with connecting up to 8 units of FX PLC.
  2. 2.The bit device (0 to 64 points) and word device (4 to 8 points) have been automatically linked among each station. The ON/OFF state of other stations and data register values can be obtained by the device allocated on the local station.
