Introduction to MDU Breakers

These circuit breakers include a measuring display unit (MDU) for measuring, displaying, and sharing electric circuit information for improved energy savings in a space-saving design that reduces construction work.

The broad model lineup supports a variety of networks (CC-Link communication, MODBUS communication, electric energy pulse output), so they can be used in a wide range of applications, from managing energy-saving efforts to monitoring power distribution equipment.
