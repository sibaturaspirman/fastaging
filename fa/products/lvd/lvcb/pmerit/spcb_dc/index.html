Product Lineup

DC is more difficult to cut off than AC because there is no current zero point, and standard products are not available for DC high voltages. For this reason, use a circuit breaker for DC special voltages.
4-pole products are available for 550VDC and 600VDC. (The NF1250-SDW is a 3-pole product.)
