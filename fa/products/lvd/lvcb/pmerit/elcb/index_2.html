CE and CCC Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers Supporting the Use of Three-Phase Power Supplies

The China GB standard GB14048.2 2008 edition was established, and in the same way as European EN standards, it requires that “the function as an earth leakage circuit breaker must operate normally even when there is an open phase”.
With the WS-V series, CE and CCC earth leakage circuit breakers support the use of three-phase power supplies.

Lineup of Models Supporting Harmonics and Surges is Also Available

Compared to ordinary products, these earth leakage circuit breakers have earth leakage detection characteristics that make unnecessary earth leakage tripping less likely in the event of harmonics and power surges. For harmonics, an active filter is installed in the electronic circuit for earth leakage detection. This excludes superimposed harmonics without impairing the detection of minute earth leakage signals in the fundamental frequency. For this reason, it is an earth leakage detection system that is resistant to the effects of harmonic earth leakage current.

For power surges, an original Mitsubishi DPDC surge identification circuit is used, preventing unnecessary activation caused by leakage current flowing to the surge absorber that is installed grounded to protect against induced surges and overvoltage without loss of ground fault current detection performance.
