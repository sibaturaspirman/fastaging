Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker

An earth leakage circuit breaker is a circuit breaker that is responsible for shutting off the circuit before an electric shock accident or fire occurs as a result of earth leakage.
Its common abbreviation is ELCB (Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker), and it is indicated as “NV” in our company’s model names.

Structure of an Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker

Our earth leakage circuit breaker is generally composed of a ZCT (Zero-phase-sequence Current Transformer) + Earth leakage relay + Circuit breaker.
The ZCT detects earth leakage current, notifies the electronic circuit, and shuts off the circuit using an internal tripping device.

Necessity of Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers

Dangers of earth leakage

A person feels tingling when a current of less than 5 mA is applied to the human body, however this range does not produce life-threatening effects. When a current of between 5 mA and 20 mA is applied, the motor nerves are paralyzed and the person becomes unable to move. If this condition continues, it puts the person in danger. When a current of between 20 mA and 100 mA is applied, irregularities in heart function occur, and it endangers the person’s life. A leakage current of 100 mA or more can cause a fire.
An earth leakage circuit breaker detects earth leakage of approximately 15 to 30 mA, and immediately shuts off the circuit within 0.1 seconds, preventing an accident.
