DSN Switches

This is a switchgear with the overcurrent tripping element removed from the standard circuit breaker, and can be used as a switchgear with a large switching capacity.
The appearance, external dimensions, hole dimensions, available accessories, etc. are identical to those of C, S, and H class circuit breakers.
