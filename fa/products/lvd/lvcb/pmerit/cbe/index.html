Circuit Protectors

These are used primarily for protection of low-capacity control circuits, heaters, motors, solenoid valves, transformers, electronic circuits, and other components that cannot be protected by an MCCB. They help to save space in the control circuits of mechanical equipment, control panels, and other equipment.
A lineup of multiple products is available with precise rated current types and overcurrent activation characteristics.

Also supports spring clamp terminal types!

AC/DC Common Use Type

Available for both AC and DC

The products can be used for circuits up to 250VAC, 65VDC (1P), and 125VDC (2P) as it is.
This enables reduced storage space and less management work.
There is no concern about incorrect AC/DC installation. (3-pole products and products with inertial delay are for AC only.)

Contributes to Space-Saving!
