KC Series

The external dimensions of 30 to 100A frame models are suitable for distribution panel applications with JIS-compliant module dimensions (height of 60mm), and the IEC 35mm rail standard compatibility also makes these suitable for control panel applications.
In addition to molded case and earth leakage circuit breakers, we also offer earth leakage alarm breakers and circuit breakers with neutral wire open-phase protection for single-phase 3-wire circuits to meet various customer needs.
