Introduction

The AnyWire ASLINK wiring system can be incorporated in MELFA robots, to resolve gripper wiring problems. (*Supported Robots: FR series, F series)
(ASLINK:Manufactured by AnyWire: Exclusively for Mitsubishi Electric robots)

Please refer to the Catalog for details.

Features

By introducing AnyWire ASLINK...
By connecting the AnyWire dedicated cable unit to the standard wiring of a conventional robot, all 256 I/O points of the robot gripper can be used without drawing external wiring to the robot arm.

Compatible models: RV-4FR/7FR/13FR/20FR series (excluding -SH specifications)
