Geared motors are drive sources contributing to energy saving and are useful in a wide range of industries.

These motors comply with IE3 class of international standard IEC60034-30 (0.75kW or more). We ensured compatibility with conventional products by performing optimization using high-efficiency technologies.
S-PM geared motors are sensorless servo series that adopt magnet motor, and these motors make further contributions to energy saving.
