The energy saving calculation system

This system enables you to check and see the energy saving effect when operating a FREQROL series inverter coupled with a 3-phase motor (a general purpose motor or an IPM motor).

  • Entering the operating conditions is all you have to do. The system will simply take care of the rest to simulate the energy saving effect.
  • Simple and convenient to simulate the energy saving effect before introducing an inverter.
  • Choose the constant torque application or fan/pump application according to your purpose.
  • The graph indicates the energy saving break point and life cycle cost in a simple, comprehensible manner.
