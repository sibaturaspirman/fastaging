FR-F700PJ

Compact inverter for fans and pumps
Both the general-purpose motor and the IPM motor can be operated. The general-purpose motor driving setting can be switched to IPM driving setting by only one setting.
When the budget is limited, equipment investment can be made in several stages. Use this inverter with a general-purpose motor at first, then with an IPM motor.
