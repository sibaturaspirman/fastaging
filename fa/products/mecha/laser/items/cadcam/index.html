LA series

CAD/CAM system for 2D laser processing machines
CAD/CAM system dedicated to Mitsubishi Electric laser machines:
Feaures easy-to-use, fast and efficient nesting. Optimizes laser processing capabilities.
The LA series improves the efficiency of the total production process by utilizing abundant functionalities in connection with Mitsubishi Electric laser processing machines and related application software.

TL-II series

CAD/CAM system for 3D laser processing machines
The TL-II series allows for offline teaching to be carried out on the computer. This enables data generation and laser processing to be performed at the same time.
Also equipped with easy-to-use features such as automatic collision avoidance, jig generation and processing simulation.

Lantek Flex3d Tubes

CAD/CAM system for tube processing on 2D and 3D laser processing machines
Offers designing, nesting, cutting and NC program generation of tubes and pipes. Round and square tubes are standard while angles and channels are optional.
