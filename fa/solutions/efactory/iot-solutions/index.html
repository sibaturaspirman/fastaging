For those who are considering introducing IoT

You may face the following challenges when implementing IoT in your organization.

  • ・Not knowing where to start
  • ・The effects of investment in IoT are unclear, making it difficult to

determine where the next investment should be made. Mitsubishi Electric sets the items to be managed (KPI) when implementing IoT in shop floors, utilizes the evaluation indicators "SMKL" for each KPI to understand the current progression of IoT, and sets the future goals.

With this indicator, you can understand the products and solutions required to reach your goals, and how much investment is required.

Means to Achieve IoT

Select the IoT level you aim to achieve from 16 elements of SMKL, and confirm the products and solutions required to achieve the goal.
