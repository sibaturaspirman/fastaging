Precise screw tightening

Two robots work together to transfer the flexible workpiece (FPC: flexible printed circuit) and tighten the screws. Force sensors are used to detect position shifts and abnormalities to prevent damage.

Precision assembly of parts (memory) through

One robot removes and inserts memory cards. Force sensors are used to sense pressure and detect abnormalities and assembly completion.

Precision assembly of parts (HDD) through

One robot grasps server HDDs and installs them into the server. Force sensors enable assembly in deep locations which would be difficult using only vision sensors.
