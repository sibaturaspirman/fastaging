Maintenance

Device level

Issues

The tendency of defects to appear in resin molding isn’t well understood, leading to loss costs as the occurrence of continuous defects goes unnoticed.

Solution

Analyze and diagnose feature quantities to prevent plastic molding machine defects
from continuously occurring

Using the data collected with MELIPC industrial-use PC in the MELSOFT MaiLab (data analysis software), feature quantities from parameter waveform data that may affect molding quality are selected, the association with defects is analyzed, and diagnostic rules are created. By collecting molding machine data in real time with MELIPC and diagnosing it based on rules using MELSOFT MaiLab, signs of molding defects are identified and maintenance can be performed before multiple defects occur.

Benefits

The costs of losses due to waste is reduced by detecting signs of molding defects.
