Case04To analyze the energy loss of production facilities and reduce CO2 emissions

  • Visualization
  • Analysis
  • Diagnosis
Issues
We want to reduce the amount of CO2 emitted during production, but we don't even have time to check the data in the first place, so we don't know where the waste is.
Solution
  • AI automatically extracts energy loss for each device process and its associated causes
  • Improvement activities are implemented based on diagnosis results of the automatically extracted loss factors

EcoAdviser's AI function provides a solution for CO2 emissions loss analysis and diagnosis

Benefits
  • Through automatic analysis, AI finds the waste hidden in production equipment difficult for humans to detect
  • AI identifies loss factors then implements appropriate countermeasures, thus reducing labor time conventionally required for such tasks
